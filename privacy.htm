Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

At Choicease, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we do not collect, and how your data is handled while using our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Choicease is a simple, static web application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as your name, email address, or browsing history.

2. User Data

All work you create using Choicease remains entirely on your device. We do not have access to your data. If you choose to export or share your work in different formats, that process happens locally in your browser.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party trackers.

4. Third-Party Services

Choicease is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect minimal technical information such as IP addresses and request logs for security and operational purposes, in accordance with their own Privacy Statement.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@choicease.com.
