🪜 Our Simple Steps:

  1. Kick off: Tell us your decision (or import one!).
  2. List options: Note down your choices.
  3. Set criteria: What matters in making the choice?
  4. Weigh 'em: Rate how important each criterion is.
  5. Score options: How do they measure up on these criteria?
  6. Results time: See the winners, rankings + breakdowns.
  7. Share/Save: Export or post on r/choicease for vibes.
